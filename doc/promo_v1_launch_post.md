## English (LinkedIn)

🚀 **Marketing Agent Team v1.0 is live!**

I built an open-source AI agent team that turns one product category into a complete go-to-market starting point: a team of research agents (trend scout, market analyst, audience researcher, competitor analyst, predictor) finds and ranks the products most likely to take off, then a sourcing agent finds quality-first, EU-certified suppliers for the winners — all orchestrated as a LangGraph StateGraph on the Claude Agent SDK, with a live dashboard, target-market scoping (country/province/town), reports in English/中文/Español, and PDF export.

It's **MIT licensed** — free to use, even commercially.
